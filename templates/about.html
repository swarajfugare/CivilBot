About CivilAI Assistant

Your intelligent companion for civil engineering design, estimation, and site safety.

Empowering Civil Engineers with AI

CivilAI Assistant is a comprehensive web application designed specifically for civil engineers, construction professionals, and students. Built with cutting-edge AI technology, it provides intelligent assistance for structural design, material estimation, project scheduling, and safety analysis.

Our mission is to streamline engineering workflows, reduce calculation time, and enhance decision-making through AI-powered insights and automated calculations based on established engineering practices and standards.

Key Features
AI Chat Assistant

Get instant answers to complex civil engineering questions, IS code references, and technical guidance.

Structural Calculator

Calculate beam dimensions, reinforcement requirements, and material quantities for structural elements.

BOQ Generator

Generate detailed Bill of Quantities with material estimates and cost calculations.

Project Scheduler

Create project timelines with Gantt charts and AI-powered schedule optimization.

Safety Analysis

Upload site images for AI-powered safety compliance analysis and risk assessment.

Data-Driven Insights

Make informed decisions with AI analysis and recommendations based on engineering best practices.

Standards & Compliance

Our calculations and recommendations are based on established engineering standards including:

  • IS 456:2000 - Plain and Reinforced Concrete
  • IS 800:2007 - General Construction in Steel
  • IS 1893:2016 - Earthquake Resistant Design
  • IS 875:1987 - Code of Practice for Design Loads
  • NBC 2016 - National Building Code of India
  • International Safety Standards
Important Disclaimer

Professional Consultation Required: CivilAI Assistant is a design aid tool that provides preliminary calculations and guidance. All designs, calculations, and recommendations should be verified by qualified professional engineers before implementation.

  • This tool does not replace professional engineering judgment
  • Local building codes and regulations may vary
  • Site-specific conditions must be considered in final designs
  • Structural analysis should be performed by licensed engineers
  • Safety analysis is for guidance only and requires professional inspection
